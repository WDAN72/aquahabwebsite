Our Environmental Consulting Services

A comprehensive suite of services to meet your project's needs in Nova Scotia, from planning and permitting to completion.

Fish Passage Assessment & Design

Analysis of barriers like failing culverts and expert design of solutions to restore fish connectivity and meet DFO requirements.

Habitat Assessment & Offsetting

Detailed fish habitat assessments and design of effective, compliant fisheries offsetting and compensation plans.

Wetland Assessment & Delineation

Professional wetland assessment and delineation for provincial wetland permitting and regulatory compliance in Nova Scotia.

Drone Surveying & GIS

High-resolution aerial imagery, topographic mapping, and advanced GIS analysis for site planning and habitat monitoring.

Watercourse Alterations

Certified expertise in the planning, design, and permitting of watercourse alterations to meet provincial and federal regulations.

Professional Training

Customized training on fish habitat assessment, regulations, and best practices for watercourse alteration and connectivity.
