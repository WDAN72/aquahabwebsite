Your Partner in Environmental Compliance and Ecological Design

Based in Nova Scotia, AquaHab Environmental Services provides innovative and practical solutions for projects impacting aquatic environments. We partner with our clients to navigate the complex regulatory landscapes of DFO, NSECC, and municipal governments, ensuring your project is both compliant and ecologically responsible.

Our expertise in fisheries science, wetland delineation, and habitat restoration allows us to deliver results that meet the requirements of the Fisheries Act and support your project's success.

Our Core Environmental Services

A comprehensive suite of services for linear developments, construction, and land use planning in Nova Scotia.

Fish Passage Assessment & Design

We assess barriers to fish passage like culverts, dams, and other obstacles, and design and implement effective solutions.

Habitat Assessment & Offsetting

Conducting detailed fish habitat assessments for DFO Requests for Review or culvert or bridge construction. We design and implement effective, compliant offsetting and habitat compensation plans.

Wetland Assessment & Delineation

Professional Wetland Delineation and/or assessment services for projects in Nova Scotia. Certified in WESP-AC. We handle provincial wetland alteration applications.

Drone Surveying & GIS

High-resolution aerial imagery, topographic mapping (Photogrammetry) and advanced GIS analysis for site planning, habitat monitoring, and more.

Watercourse Alterations & Fish Rescue

Certified expertise for watercourse alteration permits. We also provide electrofishing fish rescue services for construction dewatering and instream work.

Professional Training

Customized training for you or your team.
