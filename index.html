Bridging Development and Ecology

AquaHab Environmental Services is dedicated to providing innovative and practical solutions for projects impacting aquatic environments. We partner with our clients to navigate complex regulatory landscapes, ensuring projects are both compliant and ecologically responsible.

Our expertise in fisheries science, wetland science, and habitat restoration allows us to deliver results that support both industry and the environment.

Core Services

We offer a comprehensive suite of services to meet the needs of your project from planning to completion.

Fish Passage Assessment & Design

Comprehensive analysis of barriers to fish movement and expert design of culverts, fishways, and other passage solutions.

Habitat Assessment & Offsetting

Detailed fish habitat assessments and design of effective, compliant fisheries offsetting and compensation plans.

Wetland Assessment & Delineation

Professional wetland asssessment using industry standardized Wetland Ecosysteam Services Protocol and delineation of wetland boundaries for regulatory compliance.

Drone Surveying & GIS

High-resolution aerial imagery, topographic mapping, and advanced GIS analysis for site planning and habitat monitoring.

Watercourse Alterations

Certified expertise in the planning, sizing, and installation of watercourse alterations to meet regulations.

Professional Training

Customized training workshops and courses for teams on topics like fish habitat assessment, regulations, and best practices.
